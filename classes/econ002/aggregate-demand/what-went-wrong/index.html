What Went Wrong?

Each question shows a causal chain with exactly one broken link. Find the first step where the logic goes wrong and click it. Covers fiscal policy, monetary policy, IS curve mechanics, and aggregate demand.

How to use this app
  • Read the starting condition, then trace the chain left to right.
  • Click the first step where the logic breaks down — where what's claimed doesn't follow from the step before it.
  • Green = you found the error. Red = not quite — the correct broken step lights up green.
  • Read the explanation, then click Next → to continue.
