Aggregate Demand

Use IS–FR to see where the AD curve comes from, and build the mechanism that explains why output changes.

How this app works
  1. Click New Scenario to get a “news brief” about G, T, P, or Z.
  2. You can move sliders anytime to see how the graphs respond.
  3. Build the mechanism by dragging pills into the blanks (no hints in the blanks).
  4. Click Check mechanism.
  5. Once the slider that matches the scenario is moved in the correct direction (any magnitude), the app reveals \(Y_2\), \(r_2\), and highlights the change.
