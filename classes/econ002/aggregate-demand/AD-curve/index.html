Aggregate Demand

Predict what shifts (or moves along) IS, FR, and AD. Then build the mechanism and test it with the correct slider.

How this app works
  1. Click New Scenario (headline format).
  2. Predictions:IS, FR, and AD, choose shift vs. movement along and the direction. Click Check answers (right or wrong).
  3. Mechanism:Check mechanism. (You can do this before or after checking predictions.)
  4. Sliders:Check answers. After that, the app unlocks only the correct slider and only in the correct direction, so you can see what actually changes.
  5. Once you move the unlocked slider in the allowed direction (any magnitude), the app reveals \(Y_2\), \(r_2\), and highlights the change on IS–FR and AD.
  • Fiscal / demand shocks (G, T, C, I) shift IS → AD shifts.
  • P changes shift FR and cause a movement along AD (different point at a different price level).
  • Z shifts FR and shifts AD.
