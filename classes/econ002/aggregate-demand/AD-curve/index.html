Aggregate Demand

Predict what shifts (or moves along) IS, FR, and AD. Then build the mechanism and test it using the unlocked slider.

How this app works
  1. Click New Scenario to get a headline about G, T, C, I, P, or Z.
  2. Mechanism: Drag pills into the blanks (keyboard: Tab to a pill, press Enter or Space to select it, then Tab to a slot and press Enter or Space to place it; Delete or Backspace removes from a slot).
  3. Predictions: For IS, FR, and AD choose shift vs movement along and the direction. Click Check answers (right or wrong).
  4. Sliders: Sliders are locked until you click Check answers. After checking, the app unlocks only the correct slider and only in the correct direction.
  5. Once you move the unlocked slider in the allowed direction (any amount), the app reveals \(Y_2\), \(r_2\), and the new point on AD.
