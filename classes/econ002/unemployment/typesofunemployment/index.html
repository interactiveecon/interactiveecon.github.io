Types of Unemployment

Sort scenario cards into frictional, structural, or cyclical. Then see how these relate to the natural rate \(u^*\) and cyclical unemployment.

Key ideas
  • Frictional: search/matching, short transitions
  • Structural: mismatch (skills/location/industry changes)
  • Cyclical: weak demand in recessions
  • Natural rate: \(u^* = u_f + u_s\)
  • Cyclical (rate): \(u-u^*\)
