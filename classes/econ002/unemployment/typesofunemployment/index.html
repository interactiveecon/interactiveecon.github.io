Types of Unemployment

Sort scenario cards into frictional, structural, or cyclical. Then connect those categories to the natural rate \(u^*\) and cyclical unemployment \(u-u^*\).

Key ideas
  • Frictional: search/matching (job-to-job, new entrants)
  • Structural: mismatch (skills/location/industry changes)
  • Cyclical: weak demand in recessions
  • Natural rate: \(u^* = u_f + u_s\)
  • Cyclical: \(u - u^*\)
