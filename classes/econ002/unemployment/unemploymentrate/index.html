Unemployment Categories and Flows

Track how people moving between Employed, Unemployed, and Not in the Labor Force affects the unemployment rate and labor force participation rate.

Key definitions
  • Labor force: \(LF = E + U\)
  • Unemployment rate: \(u = \dfrac{U}{E+U}\)
  • LFPR: \(LFPR = \dfrac{E+U}{E+U+N}\)
  • Population (in this lab): \(Pop = E+U+N\)

In this lab, “Not in labor force” includes students, retirees, discouraged workers, etc.
