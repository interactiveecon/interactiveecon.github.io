Unemployment Categories and Flows

First, classify people into Employed, Unemployed, or Not in the Labor Force. Then use the flow simulator to see how \(u\) and LFPR change when people move between categories.

Key definitions
  • Labor force: \(LF = E + U\)
  • Unemployment rate: \(u = \dfrac{U}{E+U}\)
  • LFPR: \(LFPR = \dfrac{E+U}{E+U+N}\)
  • Population (in this lab): \(Pop = E+U+N\)
