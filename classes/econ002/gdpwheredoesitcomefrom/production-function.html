Production Functions

Explore constant returns to scale, marginal products, diminishing marginal product, and optimal hiring: hire until \(w/p = MPL\) (and in the short-run, choose \(L\) holding \(K\) fixed).

Model used in this lab
  • Production: \(Y = A K^{\alpha} L^{1-\alpha}\)
  • \(MPL = \frac{\partial Y}{\partial L} = (1-\alpha) A K^{\alpha} L^{-\alpha}\)
  • \(MPK = \frac{\partial Y}{\partial K} = \alpha A K^{\alpha-1} L^{1-\alpha}\)
  • Short-run hiring rule: choose \(L\) so \(w/p = MPL(K,\;L)\)
