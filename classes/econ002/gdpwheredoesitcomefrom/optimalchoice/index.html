Optimal Capital and Labor

The firm chooses \((K^*,L^*)\). At the optimum: \(MPL = W/P\) and \(MPK = R/P\). (Decreasing returns ensures a unique interior optimum.)

How this works (variables)
  • \(A\): productivity
  • \(P\): output price
  • \(W\): nominal wage
  • \(R\): nominal rental rate of capital
  • \(W/P\): real wage
  • \(R/P\): real rental rate
  • Production (DRS): \(Y = A K^{\alpha} L^{\beta}\) with \(\alpha+\beta<1\)
  • Optimality: \(MPL(K^*,L^*)=W/P\), \(MPK(K^*,L^*)=R/P\)
