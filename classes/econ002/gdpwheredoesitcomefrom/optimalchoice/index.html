Optimal Capital and Labor

With decreasing returns to scale, a firm has a unique optimal \((K^*,L^*)\). At the optimum: \(MPL = W/P\) and \(MPK = R/P\).

Variables
  • \(A\): productivity
  • \(P\): output price
  • \(W\): nominal wage
  • \(R\): nominal rental rate of capital
  • \(W/P\): real wage
  • \(R/P\): real rental rate
