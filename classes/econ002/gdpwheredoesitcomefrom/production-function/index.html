Marginal Product

Discrete (backward-difference) marginal products: MPL = F(K,L) − F(K,L−1) and MPK = F(K,L) − F(K−1,L). Sliders move in steps of 1, but output curves are shown as smooth functions.

Grey dashed curve = baseline (fixed). Solid curve = current settings. Axes always include both curves.

Sliders

K and L run from 1 to 10. A runs from 1 to 5. Step size is 1.
