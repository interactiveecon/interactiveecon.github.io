Marginal Product

Backward-difference marginal products: MPL = F(K,L) − F(K,L−1) and MPK = F(K,L) − F(K−1,L). Sliders move in steps of 1, but curves are shown smoothly.

Grey dashed curve = baseline (fixed). Solid curve = current settings. Axes start “wide” (K at max, A/L at initial) and expand if needed.
