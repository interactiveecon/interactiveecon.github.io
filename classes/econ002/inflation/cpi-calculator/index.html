CPI Basket Builder

Build a base-year basket and compute the CPI (base year = 100) and inflation.

Key formulas
  • Basket cost in year \(t\): \(\;\sum_i P_{i,t}Q_{i,0}\)
  • CPI in year \(t\): \(\;\text{CPI}_t = 100 \cdot \dfrac{\sum_i P_{i,t}Q_{i,0}}{\sum_i P_{i,0}Q_{i,0}}\)
  • Inflation from year \(t-1\) to \(t\): \(\;\pi_t = \dfrac{\text{CPI}_t-\text{CPI}_{t-1}}{\text{CPI}_{t-1}}\)

Enter base-year quantities \(Q_0\) and prices in each year.
