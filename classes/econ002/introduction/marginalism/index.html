Marginalism: Optimal Choice

Choose the quantity \(q\) that maximizes net benefit. The rule: keep going while MB ≥ MC, stop when the next unit has MB < MC.

  2. Pick a quantity \(q\) (0–10). Think “Should I do one more unit?”
  3. Click Check. The app highlights the optimal stopping point.
