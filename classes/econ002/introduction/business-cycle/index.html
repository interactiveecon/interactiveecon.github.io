Business Cycle Anatomy

Identify the peak and trough in real GDP. Then see how unemployment moves over the cycle (and lags).

Instructions
  1. Click Peak, then click on the GDP chart near the high point of the cycle.
  2. Click Trough, then click on the GDP chart near the low point.
  3. Click Check. If correct, the recession (peak → trough) is shaded on both charts.

Key terms: Expansion = rising real activity; Recession = falling real activity (peak → trough). Unemployment typically rises during recessions and often peaks after the trough (a lag).
