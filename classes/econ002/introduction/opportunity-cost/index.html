Opportunity Cost

For each scenario, choose the opportunity cost: the value of the next best alternative you give up.

Instructions
  1. Click New Scenario.
  2. Select the option that best describes the opportunity cost (the next best alternative).
  3. Click Check to see if you’re right and read the explanation.

Tip: Opportunity cost is usually not “everything you give up”—it’s the best thing you give up.

Question

Pick the opportunity cost.

—

Click “New Scenario” to begin.
How to think about it
  • List the realistic alternatives.
  • Identify the best alternative you would have chosen.
  • The value of that best alternative is the opportunity cost.

Common traps
  • Choosing an out-of-pocket payment instead of the next best alternative.
  • Picking “all of the above” when the question asks for the best alternative.
  • Picking something you still get (not actually given up).
