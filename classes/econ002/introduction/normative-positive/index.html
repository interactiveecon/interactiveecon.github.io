Positive vs Normative Economics

Drag each statement into the correct bucket. Positive = testable/factual. Normative = value judgment (efficiency, equity, growth, stability).

Instructions
  1. Click New Round to draw a random set of statements into Staging.
  2. Drag each card into Positive or one of the four Normative sub-buckets.
  3. Click Check to see which placements are correct and to read short explanations.
  4. Rule of thumb: if the statement contains words like “should,” “better,” “fair,” “too high,” or “ought,” it’s usually normative.
