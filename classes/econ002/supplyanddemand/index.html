Along vs Shift (Supply & Demand)

Predict what the scenario does (along vs shift), then predict the market outcome (excess demand/supply, price, quantity).

Key rule
  • Price of the good changes → movement along curves.
  • Anything else → a shift (demand or supply changes).
  • In this lab you must first classify the scenario correctly, then predict the outcome before the graph reveals everything.
