Move Along vs Shift (Supply & Demand)

Decide whether a scenario causes a movement along a curve or a shift of a curve. Then use the Why? panel to connect the scenario to the correct shifter.

Key rule
  • If the scenario changes the price of the good itself → movement along the existing curve.
  • If the scenario changes anything else → a shift (demand or supply changes).
  • After you answer, the graph shows a dashed “before” curve and the new curve (or a movement along).
