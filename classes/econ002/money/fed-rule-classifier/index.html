How this app works

The Fed Rule is \(r = aY + bP + cZ\). Every scenario changes exactly one of these three variables.

  1. Click New Scenario to read a news brief.
  2. Click one of the six buttons to identify which variable changed and whether it went up or down.
  3. The app immediately reveals whether you were right, explains why, and shows the causal chain.
  4. Click New Scenario to keep practicing.
  • \(Y\) (Output): changes in real activity ‚Äî production, spending, investment, exports.
  • \(P\) (Price level): changes in overall prices or inflation pressure.
  • \(Z\) (Other factors): changes in the Fed's stance due to financial conditions, uncertainty, or policy signals ‚Äî these shift the entire FR curve.
