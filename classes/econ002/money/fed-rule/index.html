Fed Rule

Predict whether a scenario causes a movement along the Fed rule or a shift of the Fed rule.

How this app works
  1. Click New Scenario to get a short “news brief.”
  2. Make your prediction (movement vs. shift; what happens to \(r\) and \(Y\)), then click Check.
  3. After you click Check (right or wrong), the app unlocks only the correct slider and only in the correct direction, so you are forced to see what actually changes.
  4. Move the unlocked slider in the allowed direction to reveal \(Y_2\) and/or \(r_2\) and an arrow that shows the change (the size of the move does not matter).
  5. Click Why? for the intuition behind the change.
  • Movement along: output changes → you move to a different point on the same line.
  • Shift: inflation/price pressure or other policy considerations change the Fed’s stance → the whole line shifts up/down.

Tip: If your prediction was wrong, use what you see from the unlocked slider to update your prediction and try another scenario.
