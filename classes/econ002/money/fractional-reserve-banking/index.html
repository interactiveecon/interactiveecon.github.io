T-Accounts & Money Creation

Track how an initial reserve injection creates deposits through lending.

Instructions
  1. Click New Scenario to draw an initial reserve injection and a required reserve ratio.
  2. Click Step to see what happens in the next bank: new deposits → required reserves → new loans.
  3. Watch totals: deposits (money), loans, and the implied multiplier.

Key idea: with required reserve ratio \(rr\), banks can lend out excess reserves. In the simple model, \(m=\frac{1}{rr}\) and \(\Delta M \approx \Delta R \cdot m\).
