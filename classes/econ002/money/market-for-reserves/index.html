Reserves Market: OMO & Interest on Reserves

Use OMO and IOR to see how the Fed changes the federal funds rate and the money supply.

Instructions
  1. Click New Scenario. You’ll get a shock: OMO buy/sell or IOR up/down.
  2. Make a prediction. Then click Check Predictions.
  3. If correct, controls unlock. Use Apply OMO or the IOR slider to implement the shock.
  4. Read the graphs:
    • Reserves market: vertical supply of reserves, downward demand. IOR acts like a floor for the federal funds rate.
    • Money supply: \(M = m \cdot R\). When IOR rises, banks hold more reserves, so the effective multiplier \(m\) falls.
