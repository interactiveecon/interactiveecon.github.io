Cross-Market Spillover Explorer

A shock in one market ripples into a linked market through substitution effects. Predict each step of the adjustment — which curve shifts, which direction, and what happens to price — then watch the diagram update to confirm.

How to use this lab
  1. Read the scenario and the equations printed on each diagram.
  2. The two panels show only the initial equilibrium at the start. Curves are added as you answer each question correctly.
  3. Answer questions about the shocked market first, then the spillover market.
  4. Press New Problem at any time to get a fresh scenario.
